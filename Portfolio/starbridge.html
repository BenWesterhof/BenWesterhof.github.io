Bay City’s Third Street Star Bridge District is a historic waterfront corridor that connects downtown Bay City to the Saginaw River and the iconic Veterans Memorial Bridge, commonly known as the Star Bridge. The district has long functioned as both a working gateway to the river and a public gathering space, blending maritime heritage, light industry, small businesses, and recreation along the water.

In recent years, the area has become a focal point for revitalization and placemaking efforts. Community stakeholders are interested in strengthening the district’s visual identity, improving public communication, and making the corridor more engaging for residents, visitors, and boat traffic while respecting its history and character.
