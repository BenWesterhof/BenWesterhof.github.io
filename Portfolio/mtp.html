Mt. Pleasant is a small city located in Michigan’s lower peninsula and filled with a rich and diversified history.Surrounded by lakes, rivers, forests, and healthy farmland, Mt. Pleasant offers its residents, businesses, and visitors a vibrant and safe place to live and flourish.

This project is a potential rebrand for the identity of the city as a whole. In order to achieve this, a bold and simplistic approach was taken to create a more modern and contemporary feel in order to appeal to a younger audience.

Also included is a marketing campaign in the "I Love Mount Pleasant" imagery. Hopefully giving this small town a big-town feel and a place that is worthy of visiting!
